Availability

    Yosuke Hanamura is generally available to hang out on Monday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday. He’s not available on rainy days. You can find Yosuke at school, in the Classroom Building 2F level, on weekdays. On Sundays and Holidays, you can meet him at Junes.

*Equip Magician Persona*
